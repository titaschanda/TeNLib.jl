StateEnvs: A container to store the MPS and its environment

At the lowest-level of abstraction, TeNLib defines StateEnvs to hold an MPS and its environment to be modified in place. Skip this part if you want to avoid lower-level abstraction.

                                    ProjMPO_MPS2,
                                    ProjMPOSum2,
                                    ProjMPOSum_MPS,

                                    ProjCouplingModel_MPS}}

    PH::T
end
